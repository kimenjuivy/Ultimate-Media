Our Story

Founded in 2014, Ultimate Media Ltd has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted media production companies. We specialize in corporate photography, videography, drone services, and live streaming solutions.

Our team of experienced professionals has worked with leading organizations including NCA, Kenya Airways, and the National Assembly, delivering high-quality content that tells compelling stories.
